# Phase 0 — Math Foundations

## Why Math First?

Math isn't a prerequisite you knock out once — it runs parallel to everything.  
Read 2–3 sections per week during phone time. Don't block Phase 1 waiting to finish math.

**Focus areas:** Discrete math (logic, sets, proofs) → Linear Algebra → Probability
