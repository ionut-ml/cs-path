# 📱 Daily Phone Reading Plan

> **Format:** 30-min sessions · Phone-friendly text · No IDE required  
> **Rule:** Read one section per session. Never guilt-trip about stopping mid-chapter.

---

## How to Use This

1. Pick the queue that matches your current phase
2. Read one row per session
3. Keep your place with a bookmark app (Pocket, Instapaper, or just the browser)
4. Come back tomorrow
